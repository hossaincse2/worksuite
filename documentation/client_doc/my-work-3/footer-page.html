Mews Guest Journey

Provide a connected journey for a connected guest. Mews Guest Journey provides an experience that’s both personalized and modern, increasing engagement with the right automation at the right time. 

Explore capabilities

Booking Engine

Direct bookings are best for business, so make your site instantly bookable with a flexible, industry-leading booking engine from Mews. Customize imagery and colors, localize language and currency, improve conversion with price comparison, and boost revenue by selling add-on products.

Learn more

Online Guest Services

From check-in to check-out, Mews provides a number of online services for guests to simplify and digitize their hotel stay. Mews Online Guest Services are secure, flexible and intuitive, allowing guests and staff to save time and embrace the contact-free experience.

Learn more

Self-service Kiosk

Some guests like to go their own way. Mews Kiosks shorten waiting times and boost  customer satisfaction, letting guests   check themselves in, cut their own keys, make payments , and check out. All they need is a reservation number from any booking channel.

Mews Hospitality Cloud capabilities

Mews Operations

The control centre for front and back office staff, harness automation to boosts efficiency, grow revenue, and improve the guest experience.

Mews Guest Journey

Put guests in control with a number of digital guest services from booking to check-out, and provide a stay that’s memorable as well as modern.

Mews Payments

Cut fraud, errors and awkward conversations with an integrated payment system that enables secure, instant payments throughout the guest journey.

Mews Marketplace

Integrate with hundreds of specialist hospitality tech solutions to get the most from your property. No connection fees, no waiting around.

Mews BI

Understand your property performance and your guests with visual dashboards with powerful and customizable reporting.

Mews Open API

Do things your way – connect and customize your property with bespoke or third-party software through Mews Open API.
